Custom Software is a term that is hard to understand. The development of custom software is for companies whose needs cannot be met by ”canned” or “off-the-shelf” software packages. A custom software solution may include the development of a complete system, the development of a custom bridge connecting two or more “canned” software packages so they may interact or a custom solution may be designed to interact with a “canned” package to provide a complete business package.

Owning custom software is similar to owning “canned” software. There may be updates or new versions. The difference is, those updates and new versions are dictated by your needs, not by the needs of the software maker. Therefore, you can better control your costs by not being forced into upgrades that may not be useful for your specific needs.

Whether updating an old paper-based process or designing a system to handle new operations, the first step is to evaluate your business workflow and the daily processes that are undertaken to complete various tasks throughout the business. The use of an “off-the-shelf” package could require you to change the way you run your business. By reviewing the total picture we can discover the most effective way to use technology to create a custom solution that will help you build a better business environment.

Visionary Concepts provides you with the opportunity to streamline processes and become more efficient by consolidating many of the arduous tasks that slow down your operations, effectively costing you time and money. By working as a team; listening to your requirements, analyzing your business workflow and assessing your current and future needs and goals we can work together to create a system specifically designed to handle the needs of your business.

Custom software development allows us to develop solutions tailored precisely to the way you want to run your business. Integrating custom software with other software used in day-to-day business allows for more efficient business operations, by automating processes to avoid duplication of tasks that could lead to errors. Custom solutions are limited only by imagination and knowledge.

We have the imagination and knowledge that it takes to develop custom solutions specifically designed to handle the needs of your business. It is our passion to develop a solution that will help you use technology to create a better business environment and become more successful.
