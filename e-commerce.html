Whether you need to sell your products or services to the public (B2C) or to other businesses (B2B), Ivytech has the consulting and development experience you need to do it right. We would be happy to discuss our end-to-end E-Commerce solutions that fit your business or project requirements. Please contact us for more information.



Adding E-Commerce to your web site is easy and inexpensive. Ivytech can bring you an effective, easy to use and in-expensive E-Commerce solution by adding an Electronic Payment Gateway to your current web site. An Electronic Payment Gateway facility enables you to accept credit card payments securely over the Internet. Your site will have the ability to process credit card transactions on-line with the option of having either immediate transaction processing or batch processing, which allows you to check your order before processing payment.
